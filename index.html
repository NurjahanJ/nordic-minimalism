Nordic Minimalism

Calm, spacious, natural design rooted in Scandinavian values of simplicity, functionality, and connection to nature.

Less is more, but warm—not cold. Democratic design accessible to everyone, emphasizing breathing room, natural materials, and understated elegance.

Philosophy

Lagom

Swedish concept meaning "not too much, not too little, just right." The perfect balance in all things.

Hygge

Danish coziness. Nordic design should feel warm and inviting, never cold or sterile.

Democratic Design

Beautiful, functional design accessible to everyone, not just the elite. Form, function, quality, sustainability, and affordability.

Design Principles

Natural Connection

Colors, materials, and imagery reference nature. Organic shapes, wood textures, stone, and colors found in moss, sand, and sky.

Generous Spacing

When in doubt, add more white space. Nordic design breathes. Wide margins, single columns, and vertical rhythm create calm.

Warm Neutrals

Never pure white or pure black. Always warm tones—off-whites, warm grays, soft beiges with muted natural accents.

Humanist Typography

Warm, friendly typefaces with generous line height. Geometric sans-serifs feel too cold—choose humanist alternatives.

Soft Everything

Corners, shadows, colors, hierarchy—nothing harsh or aggressive. Subtle transitions and gentle contrasts.

Functional Beauty

Form follows function, but warmly. Every element serves a purpose while contributing to overall harmony and calm.
